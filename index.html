Story

Long ago, in a sweet little land made of bread and of living cakes:

The Kingdom of Pastries is ruled by Queen Amann. The citizens of the kingdom are very grateful for their beautiful and generous leader. However, one present, a magical potion, puts the Queen under a spell, and she loses her body. A humble dandelion citizen senses some trouble, and where the Queen once stood, finds only her robes, her crown, and a recipe. A recipe to restore her body. A recipe for a kouign amann.

Our fellow dandelion rushes to her friend, a skeleton in a poncho, and convinces him to aid her in her quest to save the Queen, as well as the Kingdom of Pastries. As the loyal patriot citizen he is, the skeleton accepts to join his friend. Together, they will travel across the world to find the many ingredients necessary to revive the beloved Queen.
