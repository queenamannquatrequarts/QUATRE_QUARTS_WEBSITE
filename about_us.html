About Us

The team :

Our company is called Quatre Quarts. This name reflects both our team spirit and our way of working: like a recipe, each member brings an essential part to create a balanced and coherent project.

Quatre Quarts was founded in September as part of our academic program. We had to create groups to develop a video game, and we naturally chose to work together. We already knew each other, got along well, and were curious to see how far we could go as a team.

In October, we welcomed a new member, and today we are five, a number we are very proud of. Even though this is our first video game project, both individually and collectively, we see it as a strength. Everything is new, everything is to be learned, tested, and built together.

THE MEMBERS :

Clémence ~ Bibi Crabe Suprême

Clémence leads the technical side of the project, handling server setup and code coordination. She ensures all systems work together smoothly, maintaining the game’s stability, reliability, and efficient problem resolution.

Matteo ~ Quetzal

Matteo leads the game’s artistic direction, handling visuals, illustrations, and visual identity. He also composes original music and contributes to sound design, while developing the player movement system and a level editor.

Yasmine ~ Moon

Yasmine oversees project coordination and overall follow-up, ensuring smooth communication and team involvement. She maintains a global vision of the project, balancing creativity and organization, and contributed to the game’s AI.

Céleste ~ RxCLyx

Céleste manages project scheduling to keep development on track and leads the website’s design and management, creating a clear and engaging interface that helps players discover and understand the game.

Mary ~ Mary

Mary brings extensive video game development expertise, guiding the project’s technical direction, gameplay systems, and user interface. She thrives on tackling new challenges and contributed notably to the enemy AI.
